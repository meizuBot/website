Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information when you use Walrus's website or services. "Bot" from now on will refer to the usage of the Walrus Discord bot.

Collected Information

When you access this website, we will collect information about your device, IP address, and time zone. As you browse the website, we collect information regarding the pages you view. When using the bot, we will collect your Discord User ID. Other information will be optionally collected depending on the commands that you choose to use.

Why We Need This Information

Information collected is either needed for essential analytics of the website, or for general function of the bot.

Sharing Your Information

Your information will be shared with no person or entity outsite of the development team. We may also share your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant or other lawful request for information we receive, or to otherwise protect our rights. We will collect this information via log files which track all actions occuring on this website.

Minors

Our services are not intended for usage by persons under 13 years of age.

Modification

This Privacy Policy was last revised on July 12th, 2021. We may change this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy each time you visit the web site.

Contacting Us

If you have questions, comments, or concerns, joining the Discord server is the fastest way to get in touch with a staff member.
